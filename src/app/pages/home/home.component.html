About JustSpeak

JustSpeak is a language school that provides language lessons for those who cannot afford the standard courses and programs aimed for language learning. There are many students especially from the global south who would like to learn languages too but cannot afford, this very reason birthed JustSpeak. JustSpeak teaches basic German and Chichewa for those who would like to develop a solid foundation for the languages.
